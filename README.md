# 🛡️ GigShield — AI-Powered Income Protection for Delivery Partners

## 🚨 Problem

Food delivery gig workers frequently lose daily income due to uncontrollable external disruptions such as heavy rainfall, floods, severe pollution, and sudden zone closures. Traditional insurance models are slow, manual, and unsuitable for workers who depend on immediate earnings continuity.

GigShield addresses this gap by providing automated weekly income protection tailored to the gig economy.

---

## 👤 Persona Scenario

**Rajesh**, a Swiggy delivery partner in Mumbai, earns ₹900 per day. During monsoon floods he loses multiple working days with no financial safety net.

GigShield ensures that when such disruptions occur, his income loss is automatically detected and compensated without filing complex claims.

---

## 💡 Solution Overview

GigShield is an **AI-powered parametric insurance prototype** that:

* Calculates **weekly premiums dynamically** using hyperlocal disruption risk
* Monitors real-world signals like **weather severity**
* Automatically **triggers payout simulation** when disruption thresholds are met
* Detects **fraudulent claim behavior** using anomaly scoring
* Provides analytics for both workers and insurers

---

## ⚙️ System Workflow

Worker Onboards
→ AI Weekly Premium Calculated
→ Coverage Activated
→ External Disruption Detected (Weather / Environment)
→ Parametric Claim Triggered Automatically
→ Fraud Risk Evaluated
→ Payout Simulated

GigShield applies intelligent decision support across the workflow:

## 🌧️ Parametric Automation Logic

Claims are not manually filed.

If real-time disruption thresholds are met (example: rainfall > defined limit):

* Income loss is estimated
* Claim is auto-triggered
* Payout is simulated instantly

This creates a **zero-touch insurance experience.**

---

## 🔍 Fraud Detection Approach

GigShield identifies suspicious claims using:

* Sudden GPS location jumps
* Excessive claim frequency
* Weather mismatch validation
* Coordinated claim clustering

Risk is classified as:

* SAFE
* FLAGGED
* FRAUD
